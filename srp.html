01. Overview

The Seattle Rescue Plan website tracks City of Seattle spending of COVID-19 federal relief funds. This is a platform open to anyone who is interested in knowing how the City uses fundings in five areas of need: Housing & Homelessness, Community Well-being & Reopening Services, Supporting City Workers & Services, Community & Small Business Recovery, and Community Safety & Mental Health.

Problem Space

The goal of creating the Seattle Rescue Plan website is to show the audience with data visualizations and context to better understand the work that City of Seattle has done to help residents during the pandemic. Prior research has been done by another team, data sets were provided to me at the beginning of my internship. A few questions that our team had discussed:

a) What are the key objectives of our portal?
b) Who is the audience?
c) Which programs have sufficient data to be highlighted?
d) How to turn useful data into visualizations?
