01. Overview

JoyOS is a class project on prototyping a new operating system that targets users who want to form a healthier relationship with their phone devices. Our main focus is to create a mindful, seamless, and personalized OS that solves users' painpoints in several aspects.

Problem Space

How might young adults improve their engagement with mobile devices so that it promotes mindfulness and meaningful interactions?

02. Research

Questions asked:

  • What are some things you like and dislike about using your phone?
  • What do you do currently to promote personal wellness?
  • What does mindfullness mean to you?
  • Are there any factors regarding your OS that contribute negatively to your mental wellness?
  • Have you ever used a personal wellness application and if so, how was your experience?

Insights

Based on 7 interviews with college students in Seattle, we have found their painpoints and generated a design principle to build our mobile operating system.

a) Unhealthy relationship with their mobile devices.
b) Mobile devices are convenient, but distracting and spend much time on social media.
c) Users need a more mindful OS that faciliate wellness activities and reduce the overusage of their devices.

User Personas

Our next step was creating two user personas that helps us to identify different user groups and seeking target features that best fits them, as well as making future iterations.
