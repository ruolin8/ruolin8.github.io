A mobile Operating System that promotes wellness.

Role

Duration

Team

UX Designer

10 weeks (Oct-Dec 2021)

6 UX Designers

Overview

JoyOS is a class project that intends to build a prototype in forms of a new operating system that targets a group of users. The main focus is creating a mindful, seamless, and personalized OS that solves users' painpoints.

Problem Space

How might young adults improve their engagement with mobile devices so that it promotes mindfulness and meaningful interactions?

Research

Snapshot of the interviews

Insights

Based on 7 interviews with college students, we have found their painpoints and generated a design principle to build our OS.

1. Unhealthy relationship with their mobile devices.
2. Mobile devices are convenient, but distracting and spend much time on social media.
3. Users need a more mindful OS that faciliate wellness activities and reduce the overusage of their devices.

Design

User Personas

By creating two user personas, it helps us to identify different user groups and seeking target features that best fits them, as well as making future iterations.

Initial Sketches & Low-fidelity

These are whiteboard sketches and low-fidelity prototypes that present the ideas of our design pattern that helps us to stay consistent throughout the design process.
